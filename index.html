World's Biggest University

ESOFT Metro Campus is Sri Lanka's Largest Private Sector Higher Education Network.With roots going back to the year 2000, ESOFT has grown to be the largest private sector higher education network in Sri Lanka due to an extensive island-wide network of 40 branches which create opportunities for over 30,000 students each year. Initially starting with Computing, ESOFT today is a diversified education provider in the fields of ICT & Computing, Business Management, Hospitality Management, Engineering, Personal & Professional Development, Language Training and Corporate Training.
